MEET THE OWNER

A Word From Dennis Udonga

I started Udonga Furnished Homes in Kisumu in 2020 with one simple goal — to give travelers, professionals, and families a real home away from home. Not a cold hotel room, but a warm, fully furnished space where you can cook, relax, work, and live.

What began as a single unit in Kisumu has grown into 11 properties across two cities, with Mombasa on the horizon. Every home in our portfolio is personally vetted — if I wouldn't stay there myself, it doesn't carry the Udonga name.
